Where My Walk Began

The journey of DLM Christian Lifestyle began after Daniel Maritz faced a life-changing tragedy — the passing of his brother. In that moment of deep loss, Daniel encountered the transforming grace of Jesus Christ and surrendered his life completely to Him. From that encounter, a divine call was born: to share the truth of God’s Word with the world in love and authenticity.

What we believe in?

We are a non-denominational ministry and rely on scripture to guide our dogma as the final authority. We do believe that God can guide every believer through the Logos and Rhema Word, but the Rhema will never contradict the Logos. Therefore the Logos (Bible) is the final authority on all doctrine, sola scriptura!
SOLA SCRIPTURA Sola scriptura is from the Latin: sola having the idea of “alone,” “ground,” and “base,” and the word scriptura meaning “writings”—referring to the Scriptures (Bible).
We believe Scripture alone is authoritative for the faith and practice of every reborn Christian. The Bible is all we need because it is complete and true. All Scripture is breathed out by God and profitable for teaching, for reproof, for correction, and for training in righteousness, that the man of God may be complete, equipped for every good work.
~ 2 Timothy 3:16-17 ~
